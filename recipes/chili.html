Simple Chili

Description:

This recipe will guide you through making simple beef chili.

Ingredients:

  • One pound ground beef
  • One can black beans
  • One can diced tomatoes
  • One pack of chili seasoning

Cooking Instructions:

  1. In large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. It usually takes about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Once beef is browned, add chili seasoning packet and mix well.
  3. Add the cans of beans and diced tomatoes, do not drain.
  4. Simmer in pot for 10 minutes.
  5. Let stand for 5 minutes as the chili will be very hot.
  6. Serve with garnishes such as:
    • Diced onions
    • Sour cream
    • Shredded cheese
    • Cilantro
